LabView

From National Instruments, LabView is is systems engineering software for applications that require test,
measurement, and control with rapid access to hardware and data insights. For the SGTB, it is a virtual
grid simulator for visualizing various phases in the electrical grid and a safe environment for
simulating the effects of fault generation on the grid.
